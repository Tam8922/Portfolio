About me

Hello, I'm Tanpreet Dhani! I am a passionate psychology graduate and a UX/UI designer who's always ready for a challenge. With a keen understanding of user motivations and cognitive processes, which I believe is instrumental in crafting user-centric experiences, I am dedicated to craft intuitive designs that resonate with users on both cognitive and emotional levels.

I am a team player excited to leverage my collaboration skills to contribute constructively to design projects, fostering environments where collective creativity can flourish.

Currently based in the UK, but available for online collaboration and projects, if need be. I am eager to apply this interdisciplinary approach to create impactful experiences.

Fun fact!

I am from Moshi, Tanzania in the beautiful continent of East Africa. I grew up between the UK and Tanzania, having moved back and forth between the two contries, but ultimately finishing school in Tanzania.

Two things I know I always took for granted being in Africa, a. the weather (always hot or warm, maybe jacket weather on a rainy day) and b. having the wonderful view of Mt. Kilimanjaro every morning on my way to school. My little town Moshi always had a saying, which goes, "If it rains down in Moshi then it snows on the peak of Kilimanjaro".

Hope you enjoy this picture of Mt. Kilimanjaro :)
