EasyFits Avatar Creation Design

Coming into EasyFits, alongside the website, I was given the task was to design of the avatar cration process, which would allow users to create their own custom avatar to use to try on clothes in a virtual fitting room when shopping online. Not only was this crucial, but it is one of the main products EasyFits has to offer. My job was to understand how we could get users to complete this process as efficiently as possible and where they would need guidance, should they feel confused. From this, I could begin the process of desinging and prototyping wireframes to discuss and share with dev team to implement onto the website.

EasyFits website revamp

A complete redesign of the website. The previous WordPress website was not very user friendly, so the solution was to start from scratch. My role as a UX/UI designer was to ideate and prototype wireframes, ensuring that CTAs are carefully and strategically placed, information on the pages, particularly the homepage, was clear and effective, and giving users an action to complete (sign-up).

My Skills

User-centered Design

With user motivations being a priority, I conduct thorough user research including conducting interviews and surveys and creating empathy maps and user personas, to develop solutions addressing users' needs and pain points. For example, designing a travel app with the purpose of allowing users to find local experiences at their choice of destination required thorough research, which is why I conducted five 1v1 interviews with participants who met the appropriate criteria. Followed by a multidisciplinary approach, with the help of my psychology background, I conducted an in-depth analysis by translating interview data into codes and organising themes. After completing the user research with empathy maps and user personas, I was careful to implement prioritised features into the app.

Visual Design

Creating visually appealing effective user interfaces, with keen attention to detail to typography, colour and iconography. All the whilst maintaining true brand identity. For example, working as a team, I helped redesign a non-profit website using user testing data and discussions with stakeholders. It was important to the stakeholders that their website didn't lose its identity, therefore, using appropriate clean typography, simple colours and local culturally patterns was crucial to relay their message.

Prototyping and Interaction Design

Creating and prototyping wireframes with user flows effectively communicating design concepts. Additionally, creating interactive components where necessary. Examples in case studies.
